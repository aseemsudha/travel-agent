Assi Ghat Temple in Varanasi is peaceful during sunrise and attracts spiritual visitors.

Durga Temple in Varanasi is less crowded early morning and is popular among locals.

Sankat Mochan Temple in Varanasi is dedicated to Lord Hanuman and is busiest on Tuesdays.

Meenakshi Temple in Madurai is one of the most famous temples in India and attracts large crowds during the day.

Padmanabhaswamy Temple in Kerala is known for its architecture and strict temple entry rules.
